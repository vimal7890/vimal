Province of the Anglican Church of Burundi

Intro

The Province of the Anglican Church of Burundi is the Anglican Communion province in Burundi. It became a distinct province in 1992 after earlier regional links with neighboring Anglican churches, and today it is led by the Archbishop and Primate of Burundi.

Source: Intro adapted from the Wikipedia article "Province of the Anglican Church of Burundi". Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Structure

The province follows a standard Anglican episcopal structure of parishes gathered into dioceses. The archbishop remains diocesan bishop of one see while also serving as metropolitan and primate for the whole province.

Wikipedia lists nine dioceses in the current provincial structure.
