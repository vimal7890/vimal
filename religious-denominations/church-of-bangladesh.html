Church of Bangladesh

Intro

The Church of Bangladesh is a united Protestant church that brings together traditions rooted mainly in Anglican and Presbyterian life in Bangladesh. It is part of both the Anglican Communion and the World Communion of Reformed Churches.

Structure

The Church of Bangladesh is governed through a synod while also retaining bishops and dioceses in a recognizably Anglican structure. The bishop of Dhaka normally serves as moderator and primatial figure for the whole church.

Its present diocesan framework consists of three dioceses spread across Bangladesh.

Diocese Current bishop Notes
Dhaka Samuel Sunil Mankhin Metropolitan diocese and usual see of the moderator
Kushtia Hemen Halder Created from Dhaka diocese in 1990
Barisal Shourabh Pholia Newest diocese, formed in 2017
