Ruthenian Byzantine Catholic Church

Intro

The Ruthenian Byzantine Catholic Church is covered on Wikipedia as the Ruthenian Greek Catholic Church, an Eastern Catholic church rooted in the Carpathian region that uses the Byzantine Rite and includes communities in Europe and North America.
