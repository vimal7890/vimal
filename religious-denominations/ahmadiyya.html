Ahmadiyya

Intro

Ahmadiyya, officially the Ahmadiyya Muslim Jama'at or Ahmadiyya Muslim Community, is an Islamic messianic movement that originated in British India in the late 19th century and was founded by Mirza Ghulam Ahmad.
