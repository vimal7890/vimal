Macedonian Greek Catholic Church

Intro

The Macedonian Greek Catholic Church is an Eastern Catholic church that uses the Macedonian language in the Byzantine liturgy and is organized as a single eparchy.

Structure

The Macedonian Greek Catholic Church has only a single eparchy: the Macedonian Catholic Eparchy of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary in Strumica-Skopje.

As of 2017, the church had 11,374 members.

Eparchy
Macedonian Catholic Eparchy of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary in Strumica-Skopje, North Macedonia 🇲🇰
