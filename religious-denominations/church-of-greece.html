Church of Greece

Intro

The Church of Greece (Greek: Ἐκκλησία τῆς Ἑλλάδος, romanized: Ekklisía tis Elládos, lit. 'Church of Greece' or Ελλάδος Ellados), part of the wider Greek Orthodox Church, is one of the autocephalous churches that make up the communion of Eastern Orthodox Christianity.
