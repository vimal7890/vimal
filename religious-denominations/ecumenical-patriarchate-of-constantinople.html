Ecumenical Patriarchate of Constantinople

Intro

The Ecumenical Patriarchate of Constantinople (Greek: Οἰκουμενικὸν Πατριαρχεῖον Κωνσταντινουπόλεως, romanized: Oikoumenikón Patriarkhíon Konstantinoupóleos) is one of the fifteen to seventeen autocephalous churches that together compose the Eastern Orthodox Church.
