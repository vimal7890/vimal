Seventh-day Adventists

Intro

Seventh-day Adventists, officially the Seventh-day Adventist Church, are an Adventist Christian denomination distinguished by their observance of Saturday as the Sabbath and by their emphasis on the imminent Second Coming of Jesus Christ.
