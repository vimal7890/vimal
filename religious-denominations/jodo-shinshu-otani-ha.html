Jodo Shinshu (Otani-ha)

Intro

Jodo Shinshu (Otani-ha) corresponds to Otani-ha on Wikipedia, a Japanese Buddhist movement within the Jodo Shinshu or True Pure Land tradition whose headquarters are in Kyoto.
