Malankara Marthoma Syrian Church

Intro

The Malankara Mar Thoma Syrian Church, often shortened to Mar Thoma Church, is an autonomous Oriental Protestant Christian church based in Kerala, India.

Structure

For administrative purposes, the Malankara Mar Thoma Syrian Church is divided into 13 dioceses, often called Bhadhrasanams, each headed by a Metropolitan or an Episcopa.
