Maronite Church

Intro

The Maronite Church is an Eastern Catholic church rooted in the Syriac tradition, centered in Lebanon, and led by the Maronite Patriarch while remaining in full communion with the pope.

Structure

The head of the Maronite Church is the Patriarch of Antioch and the Whole Levant, who is elected by the Maronite bishops and resides in Bkerke, near Jounieh, north of Beirut, with a summer residence in Dimane.

Wikipedia describes the Maronite Church as having twenty-eight eparchies and patriarchal vicariates across the Middle East and the wider diaspora. Below is a regional list of those jurisdictions.
