Church of the Province of the Indian Ocean

Intro

The Church of the Province of the Indian Ocean is an Anglican Communion province serving Madagascar, Mauritius, and the Seychelles. It is led by its own archbishop and primate, based within one of the province's dioceses, and represents the main Anglican structure across those island territories.

Structure

The province is organized as eight dioceses: six in Madagascar and one each in Mauritius and the Seychelles. Its archbishop also remains a diocesan bishop; the current primate is also Bishop of Fianarantsoa.

This layout reflects the church's island geography. Madagascar accounts for most dioceses, while the two smaller island states each form a single diocese of their own.
