Lutheran World Federation

Intro

The Lutheran World Federation is a global communion of Lutheran churches founded in 1947 and headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland.
It coordinates shared work among member churches in theology, ecumenical relations, humanitarian service, mission, and development.
Its member churches are in pulpit and altar fellowship and span countries across every inhabited continent.
