Armenian Apostolic Church

Intro

The Armenian Apostolic Church (Armenian: Հայ Առաքելական Սուրբ Եկեղեցի, romanized: Hay Arakelakan Surb Yekeghetsi) is the national church of the Armenian people and one of the most ancient Christian institutions.
