Armenian Apostolic Church

Intro

The Armenian Apostolic Church (Armenian: Հայ Առաքելական Սուրբ Եկեղեցի, romanized: Hay Arakelakan Surb Yekeghetsi) is the national church of the Armenian people and one of the most ancient Christian institutions.

Source: Intro adapted from the Wikipedia article "Armenian Apostolic Church". Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Structure

According to The Armenian Church by Archdeacon Dowling published in 1910, the Armenian Apostolic Church was composed of four patriarchal provinces, comprising at that date seventy-two, six, and two dioceses in Turkey, Russia, and Iran, respectively.
