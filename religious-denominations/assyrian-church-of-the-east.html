Assyrian Church of the East

Intro

The Assyrian Church of the East is an Eastern Christian denomination in the East Syriac tradition with historical roots in Mesopotamia and a global diaspora.

Structure

The Assyrian Church of the East is organized into archdioceses and dioceses across the Middle East, India, Europe, North America, and Oceania.

The church is headed by Catholicos-Patriarch Mar Awa III. Below is a list of the archdioceses and dioceses named in the referenced Wikipedia structure section.
