Sanatan Dharma Maha Sabha

Intro

Sanatan Dharma Maha Sabha (सनातन धर्म महासभा), also known as the Sri Krishna Janmabhoomi Mukti Nyas or Sanatan Dharam Mahasabha, is a Hindu temple management and advocacy organization in India.
