Indian Orthodox Church

Intro

The Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church (MOSC), also known as the Indian Orthodox Church (IOC), is an autocephalous Oriental Orthodox church headquartered in Devalokam, Kottayam, in the state of Kerala, India.
