Buddhist Traditional Sangha of Russia

Intro

Buddhist Traditional Sangha of Russia (Russian: Буддийская традиционная сангха России, romanized: Buddiyskaya traditsionnaya sangkha Rossii) is a Tibetan Buddhist centralized religious organization, which is the largest Tibetan Buddhist community in Buryatia and one of the largest in Russia.
