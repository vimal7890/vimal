Slovak Greek Catholic Church

Intro

The Slovak Greek Catholic Church is an Eastern Catholic church in Slovakia that uses the Byzantine Rite and is organized around a single metropolitan province.

Source: Intro adapted from the Wikipedia article "Slovak Greek Catholic Church". Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Structure

Slovakia:

  • Archeparchy of Prešov with the following suffragans:
    • Eparchy of Bratislava
    • Eparchy of Košice
