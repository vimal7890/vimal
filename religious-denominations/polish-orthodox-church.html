Polish Orthodox Church

Intro

The Polish Orthodox Church, officially the Autocephalous Orthodox Church in the Republic of Poland (Polish: Polski Autokefaliczny Kościół Prawosławny, lit. 'Polish Autocephalous Orthodox Church'; PAKP), is one of the autocephalous Eastern Orthodox churches in full communion with the rest of Eastern Orthodoxy.
