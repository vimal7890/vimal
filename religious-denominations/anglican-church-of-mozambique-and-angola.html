Anglican Church of Mozambique and Angola

Intro

The Anglican Church of Mozambique and Angola is the Anglican Communion province for Angola and Mozambique. It was created in 2021 from dioceses that had belonged to the Anglican Church of Southern Africa, making it one of the Communion's newest provinces.

Source: Intro adapted from the Wikipedia article "Anglican Church of Mozambique and Angola". Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Structure

Wikipedia describes the province as a bi-national Anglican structure with dioceses in both Mozambique and Angola, overseen by a presiding bishop and provincial synod. The dioceses include older sees inherited from Southern African Anglican structures as well as several newer missionary dioceses created around the province's formation.

The current diocesan layout listed on Wikipedia is summarized below.
