Ethiopian Catholic Church

Intro

The Ethiopian Catholic Church is an Eastern Catholic church based in Ethiopia that uses the Alexandrian Rite in Ge'ez and is in communion with the Holy See.

Structure

There are four eparchies (bishoprics) in the country:

  • Archeparchy of Addis Abeba (metropolitan see)
  • Eparchy of Adigrat
  • Eparchy of Bahir Dar–Dessie
  • Eparchy of Emdeber
