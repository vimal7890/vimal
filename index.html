About Me

Hi, I'm Vimal! I'm an International Politics graduate with a passion for data-driven research and transparency. My work focuses on tracking censorship patterns, analysing political trends, and exploring how information flows (or is restricted) across different political systems.

I believe in the power of data to illuminate important stories about press freedom, government transparency, and democratic accountability. Through my research and projects, I aim to make complex political dynamics more accessible and understandable.
